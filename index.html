Is anyone here?
Web and User Friendly Motion Detector

The Stamford Innovation Center creates an environment where small business owners, consultants, techies, designers, and entrepreneurs can gather, collaborate, and innovate.

This website aims to provide Center visitors with a tool to identify the perfect working space, utilizing technology from flowthings.io to detect the motion activity of each room. Do you want to talk to people? Go to a room where motion was sensed! Need quiet time? Go to a room where there isn't any!
